Real life Quotes

Life Quotes by famous personalities that will make you bang your head full force into a new reality.

LIFE – is a four-letter word that is complicated enough that no one will ever decode its true meaning.
Whether you were born into a wealthy family or a poor one, your life will be full of ups and downs.
The goal in life is to minimize the down’s and live a happy and inspiring existence.
If you are down now or just wanting to read some life advice, we’ve got you covered.
These life quotes will inspire you to the core and help you live a quality and content life.

1. If life were predictable, it would cease to be life, and be without flavor. — Eleanor Roosevelt

>Some people wish that their life will work out the way they want it to be.
But let me ask you a simple question, do you like to watch a movie that has a predictable plot? My guess is, NO.

2. Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving. — Albert Einstein

If you stop, you fail.

3. Nothing can dim the light that shines from within. — Maya Angelou

The only thing that you have control over is your mind and your body.
Everything else is decided by a bunch of people and external conditions.
If you are truly happy, no one else can snatch it from you. One of the most inspirational life quotes.

4. You get in life what you have the courage to ask for. — Oprah Winfrey

One of the best life quotes on the list.
When you were a baby, you had to cry to get what you want. If you didn’t,
your parents probably thought you don’t want it. The same applies when you grow up,
the world doesn’t just hand you what you want unless you ask and fight for it.
